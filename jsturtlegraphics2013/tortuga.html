Turtle Graphics

Simple turtle graphics for kids!

Commands:
  • ad X: Advance X turtle units
  • re X: Reverse X turtle units
  • ri X: Turn right X degrees
  • le X: Turn left X degrees
  • rise: Rise the pencil
  • lower: Lower the pencil
  • rep X: Repeat X times the next commands
  • clr: Clear everything and start over
Hit F11 to go fullscreen.

Works on Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox or any other standards compliant web browser.

This tool was made with the intent to help young kids to be introduced in the art of programming.
